RanViewer Privacy Policy
======================

This Privacy Policy describes how the RanViewer App handles your information when you use the app.

1. <b>No Information Collection</b> - We do not collect, store, or process any personal information from our users. This includes names, email addresses, IP addresses, location data, or any other personally identifiable information.

2. <b>No Cookies or Tracking</b> - Our website/app does not use cookies, trackers, or any other methods to monitor your activity or gather data about you.

3. <b>No Third-Party Sharing</b> - Since we do not collect any data, we do not share any user information with third parties.

4. <b>Contact & Transparency</b> - We believe in full transparency. If you contact us directly (e.g., via a contact form or email), that information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is not stored or linked to any user profile.

5. <b>Changes to This Policy</b> - This policy may be updated, but we will always maintain our commitment to not collecting your data.

6. <b>Input files</b> - If the user uploads personal information as part of the input files (Sites and/or Documentation) this information is only stored in the app and is not used for any other purpose than to display it in the app as part of the usage of the app. The information is not shared with any third parties.

Contact [us](mailto:johan.solhed@gmail.com) If you have any questions about this policy.
